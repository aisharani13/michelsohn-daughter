Services

Michelsohn & Daughter builds everything from new construction to luxury and fully customized remodels. When designing a home or space, we work with our clients to bring out their uniqueness.

The first step in the process is a meeting with Vice President Karen Kassik-Michelsohn to discuss your dreams. Second, we conduct a feasibility study. This includes design, pricing and budget. We believe in a transparent process with no surprises. The final step is to finalize the plans and begin working.
